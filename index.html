About

Composer with a Bachelor of Jazz Studies from the University of Manitoba, where I studied performance under guitarist Larry Roy and composition from saxophonist Jon Gordon, later completing a graduate certificate in Music Composition at Humber College under composer Ari Posner and pianist Nancy Wilson.

Highlights professionally include touring with the Broadway musical "Chicago" and working as a house band guitarist for Princess Cruises based in Los Angeles and Fort Lauderdale. My work has been supported through collaborations with the Canada Council for the Arts and the Manitoba Arts Council, as well as the Screen Composers Guild of Canada, completing the SCGC mentorship program under composer Virginia Kilbertus.
