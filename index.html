Composer | Guitarist

Artur Kivilaht

Composer with work supported through collaborations with the Canada Council for the Arts and the Manitoba Arts Council, as well as the Screen Composers Guild of Canada.

Trained in music composition and performance at the University of Manitoba, later studying composition at Humber College and completing the SCGC mentorship program.

Performance highlights include touring with the Broadway musical "Chicago" and with Princess Cruises throughout the Americas.
