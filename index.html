Composer

Artur Kivilaht

Composer with work supported through collaborations with the Canada Council for the Arts and the Manitoba Arts Council, as well as the Screen Composers Guild of Canada.

Studied performance under guitarist Larry Roy and composition from saxophonist Jon Gordon at the University of Manitoba, later completing a graduate certificate in Music Composition at Humber College under composer Ari Posner and pianist Nancy Wilson and SCGC mentorship program under composer Virginia Kilbertus.

Performance highlights include touring with the Broadway musical "Chicago" and with Princess Cruises throughout the Americas.
