About me

I am a guitarist, originally from Winnipeg but now based in Ottawa. Highlights from my time playing music include touring the Prairies playing Western Swing with Woody Holler and Daniel Koulack and teaching and playing Metis trad guitar in Northern Manitoba with the Frontier School Division. I am excited to be immersing myself in Ottawa's trad and fiddle music scene.
